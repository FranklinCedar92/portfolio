About

In October of 2018, Franklin completed their MSc in Linguistics from the University of Edinburgh, in Edinburgh, Scotland. Since then, they have been a gardener, piano teacher, German tutor, product photographer, barista, gogo dancer, lumberjack, model, florist, and office manager. Franklin's initial interest in programming came from an idea regarding the intersection of natural language processing, language documentation, and sonography. They have a special knack for languages and analysis, with a bent toward creativity.
